RSS?

Make Your Own Feeds!

RSS Really Simple Syndication? Maybe? See Wikipedia. is an XML protocol that allows websites to broadcast and organize updates. Instead of relying on algorithmic feeds, RSS aggregators can build a custom feed directly from these updates.

My aggregator of choice is NewsBlur. One feature of this platform is the BlurBlog that can compile all of the stories I want to highlight and share into a feed of its own!
