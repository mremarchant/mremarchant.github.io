Smallpools, "Cycles”

A consultant at their device. Write an email to explain why teachers should attend my AI training. Sends it out.

A teacher gets an email. Paragraphs of text, generic images. Summarize this. It's an invite to a training. Paid, maybe free breakfast, might as well.

Create a presentation. Lots of bright colors and smiling faces! Make handouts. Everyone in the audience is on their laptop, maybe their phone too. Write a script, make sure to include some humor! Everyone gives a thumbs up when asked if they understand. Everyone gets paid. Lots of grading got done. Tax dollars at work. Classes are still the same.
